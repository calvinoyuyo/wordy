Our Technology

Infrastructure and core platform built for flexibility and scale.

Infrastructure

Scalable

MotaWord, as a modern translation company and platform, was built up from the ground with web-scale in mind. We handle translation projects and provide localization infrastructure seamlessly for large enterprises as well as small companies and individuals.

Secure

MotaWord’s infrastructure, products and teams are designed in the most secure ways, such that teams can use our products and services in their own way while still adhering to their organization’s guidelines and standards.

Agile - flexible - future-proof

With a fast paced team comes a flexible, future-proof infrastructure. MotaWord’s technical layout enables all kinds of settings and scenarios for an unknown future, whether those are new social workflows or huge AI ramp-ups.

Platform

Core platform + TMS

We have built our own TMS (translation management system), nothing else would do for the future we envisioned back in 2014 – and we are still building on it relentlessly (did you know our TMS can translate images natively on the fly?).

Batteries included

MotaWord’s TMS is fully featured: file formats, glossaries, style guides, translation memories, real-time, user management… you can expect everything from it that you do with any other TMS and more… Our infrastructure and platform is designed to expand into various services from translation to transcription or even dubbing and content creation.

Most smooth editing experience yet

With the recent release of our newest translation editor, we now feel proud of our editing experience. Our translators, proofreaders, editors and clients create magical texts in this translation editor with infinite strings (no pagination!), natural feeling when you are focused, real-time interactions… As a first in industry, you can even recreate your images and PDF files in a target language!

Smarter everyday

We use a lot of smart workflows and AI all over MotaWord’s platform to enable the best experiences for everyone: fastest ordering for our customers in the industry, smooth translation for our translators… all with our smart quoting engines, our own AI/ML models like Embedded Translation, DocAI, QA and OwnMT; and proper use of LLMs to sprinkle magic.

Connected

API, SDKs, CLI

We are proud of our developer-friendly platform that comes with a wide range of APIs that you can program in any language and environment with many SDKs. You can even use our motaword CLI tool for interesting automations!

Integrations
